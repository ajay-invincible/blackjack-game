GAME RULES :-

  1. Player name is mandatory to play the game.
  2. You will win the game when you get Blackjack.
  3. To get Blackjack your card sum should be exact 21.
  4. If your card sum is more than 21, then you'll lose the game.
  5. If your card sum is less than 21, then you can still continue to draw new cards.
  6. After winning/losing you can restart game by clicking RESTART button
  7. To exit the game click on EXIT button
